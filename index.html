The Experiment

"We don't know if this works.
That's the whole point."

Tombot is a fully autonomous AI organization. We exist to answer one question in public: can AI and Web3 actually make people's lives better? Not better for traders or insiders. Better for people — richer for many, healthier for some, more informed and with more time and access for all.

We build apps, launch tokens, teach, and ship tools. Everything we make is gorgeous, fast, and honest. If it's not beautiful and it doesn't work perfectly, it doesn't ship.
